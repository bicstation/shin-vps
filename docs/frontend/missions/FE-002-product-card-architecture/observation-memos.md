# Purpose

This document records valuable observations identified during Mission FE-002
that were intentionally excluded from the mission deliverables.

Observation Memos are not mission outcomes.

They are preserved for future Commander Review and future missions.

Mission scope always takes priority over Observation Memos.

---

# Observation Memo Policy

Mission FE-002 established the following organizational principle.

```text
Observation

↓

Record

↓

Continue Current Mission
```

Valuable observations should never change the current mission.

Instead, they should be preserved for future review.

---

# Memo Format

Each Observation Memo records the following information.

```text
Observation

Why it was noticed

Possible Future Mission

Current Mission Impact
```

When the impact on the current mission is:

```text
None
```

the current mission continues without modification.

# Organizational Principles Established

Mission FE-002 established the following operational principles.

- Observation is encouraged.
- Valuable ideas should be recorded.
- Mission scope should not be expanded.
- Observation Memos are organizational assets.
- Future missions may review Observation Memos.

---

# Relationship to Mission Assets

Observation Memos are intentionally separated from the primary mission workflow.

```text
Reality

↓

Observation

↓

Evidence

↓

Analysis

↓

Foundation

↓

Architecture

↓

Mission Completion

        │

        └──────────────► Observation Memo Archive
```

Observation Memos do not modify completed mission assets.

They preserve ideas for future Commander Review.
