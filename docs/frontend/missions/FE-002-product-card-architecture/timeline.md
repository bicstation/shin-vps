# Purpose

This document records the operational timeline established during Mission FE-002.

The timeline represents the approved workflow used to transform
observed Product Reality into an architectural design.

It documents the sequence of responsibilities followed throughout the mission.

---

Mission FE-002 followed the workflow below.


---

# Phase Overview

## 1. Reality

Observe the existing Product Experience.

No interpretation.

No assumptions.

Reality serves as the authoritative starting point.

---

## 2. Observation

Record the current implementation as it exists.

Focus on:

- Runtime
- Presentation
- Component Responsibility
- User Reading Flow
- Navigation

No evaluation is performed.

---

## 3. Evidence

Promote verified observations into architectural evidence.

Evidence provides the factual basis for all subsequent work.

---

## 4. Analysis

Analyze the collected evidence to identify common responsibilities,
presentation patterns, and structural relationships.

Analysis derives understanding without introducing new design.

---

## 5. Presentation Foundation

Identify reusable presentation elements shared across Product Experiences.

Foundation organizes presentation concepts without determining composition.

---

## 6. Product Card Foundation

Establish the structural building blocks of the Product Card.

The approved Foundation consists of:

```text
Card Header

↓

Card Body

↓

Information Area

↓

Action Area

↓

Card Footer
```

---

## 7. Architecture Evidence

Consolidate the evidence required to begin architectural design.

This phase confirms that sufficient knowledge has been collected
to support Architecture.

---

## 8. Product Card Architecture Design

Design Product Card Architecture using the approved workflow.

```text
Page Responsibility

↓

Required Experience

↓

Required Information

↓

Presentation

↓

Product Card Composition
```

Architecture begins from Purpose,
not from existing Product Card implementations.

---

## 9. Commander Review

Review the Architecture Design.

Confirm that:

- Mission Scope was preserved.
- Architectural principles are consistent.
- No implementation decisions were introduced.

---

## 10. Mission Asset Review

Organize all completed mission assets.

Examples:

- Observation
- Evidence
- Analysis
- Presentation Foundation
- Product Card Foundation
- Architecture Design

The purpose is organizational preservation,
not additional design.

---

## 11. Mission Completed

Mission FE-002 concludes after its outcomes
have been preserved as organizational assets.

Completion is achieved when the knowledge becomes reusable
by the Frontend Organization.

---

# Timeline Principles

Mission FE-002 established the following operational principles.

```text
Reality First.

Observe Before Explaining.

Evidence Before Design.

Foundation Before Architecture.

Architecture Before Prototype.

Complete the Mission Before Starting the Next.
```

---

# Relationship to Future Missions

This timeline documents the workflow used during Mission FE-002.

Future missions may establish different workflows
according to their own purpose.

This timeline should therefore be treated as:

- A historical record of Mission FE-002.
- An organizational reference.
- Not a universal process for every future mission.
