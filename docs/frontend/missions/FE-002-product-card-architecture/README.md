# Overview

Mission FE-002 was conducted to establish a consistent architectural approach
for Product Cards across the BIC STATION Product Experience.

The mission did not begin with component design.

Instead, it began by observing the current Product Experience,
collecting evidence, organizing common foundations,
and finally designing the Product Card Architecture
from the responsibility of each page.

Mission FE-002 has now been completed.

The artifacts produced during this mission are preserved
as organizational assets of the Frontend Experience Team.

---

# Mission Objective

The objective of Mission FE-002 was to answer one question.

> **How should a Product Card be composed to express the responsibility of each Product Experience?**

The mission focused exclusively on Product Card Architecture.

Implementation, Runtime modification,
and Frontend-wide Architecture were intentionally excluded.

# Key Architectural Principle

Mission FE-002 established the following architectural workflow.

```text
Page Responsibility

↓

Required Experience

↓

Required Information

↓

Presentation

↓

Product Card Composition
```

Product Card Architecture begins with **Purpose**.

It does not begin with an existing Product Card implementation.
